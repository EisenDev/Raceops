# Player Guide

## Welcome to TechOps: Hidden Cache Run!
You are about to embark on a physical and digital race. Your goal is to find hidden cards throughout the venue and complete your mission.

## 1. Joining the Game
1. Go to the event website on your phone.
2. Tap **Join Game**.
3. Choose a **Username** and **Password** (keep it simple!).
4. Enter your **Display Name**.
5. Select your **Team** from the list.

## 2. Your Mission
Once you join, you will see **"Your Mission"**. This is a checklist of items you need to find. 
- You typically need to find **5 specific items**.
- You can see the labels of the items you are looking for (e.g., "The Blue Cache", "Server Room Alpha").

## 3. Finding Cards
Cards are hidden everywhere! They might be under tables, on walls, or hidden in plain sight.
Each card has a **Unique Code**.

## 4. Claiming a Card
When you find a card:
1. Tap the **Scan/Enter Code** button on your dashboard.
2. Type in the code found on the card (or scan the QR code if available).
3. If it's part of your mission, it will be marked as **Collected**.

## 5. Card Types
- **Cache:** Completes your mission.
- **Bonus:** Gives you extra points!
- **Bug:** Oh no! This is a penalty. You'll lose some points.
- **Fix:** Used to remove a Bug penalty.
- **Bounty:** High value! Find these to skyrocket your score.
- **Nullify:** Find these to protect your team or reduce an opponent's bounty.

## 6. Important Rules
- **One Card, One Use:** Once a card is entered by *anyone*, it cannot be used again. Be fast!
- **Mission Only:** If you find a card that isn't on your mission, you can't claim it for mission progress (but it might be a Bonus!).
- **Final Submission:** Once you've found everything or the time is up, tap **Submit Progress**.

## 7. Troubleshooting
- **Weak Signal:** If the page isn't loading, move to an area with better Wi-Fi or LTE. Your progress is saved automatically.
- **Code Error:** Double-check the letters and numbers. `0` (Zero) and `O` (Letter O) can look similar!

Good luck, and have fun!
